The Project

Xentavo Foundation exists to help bring crypto-currencies into the digital world. We want to help blockchain technologies to lead innovation in the next years to come.

Features

  • Litecoin based blockchain
  • Identity verification
  • Highly scalable architecture
  • Enterprise level security

Developers

Developers are at the center of the Xentavo Project. We are building a simplified layer of abstraction on top of blockchain technologies to easy its adoption on the digital world.
