Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the motivation behind this project?

Initially, we set out to build a new crypto-currency. However, we quickly realized that due to time constrains and our responsibilities it was impossible for us to execute our vision. And during our journey we realized that there was a bigger need to help people learn about crypto so we decided to pivot our project into this new vision. The project was born by a few friends getting together and decided they wanted to learn about crypto currencies and explore the space. And most importantly, to have a lot of fun while doing it!


2. Why cloning Litecoin?

Litecoin is a clone of Bitcoin. However, it evolves at a faster pace while remaining as the most compatible crypto protocol. By basin Xenblocks on Litecoin we have immediate access to all the Bitcoin and Litecoin ecosystem including software and hardware wallets, exchanges support, documentation, tooling, etc. Keeping Xenblocks relevant and current with the existing crypto-currency ecosystem.


3. How many total coins will there be and how many have been pre-mined?

Xenblocks will have a total of 1 Billion coins and 500 Million have been pre-mined.


4. How are you distributing the coins?

Currently, coins are distributed in the following way:

  1. New users get 5, 50 o 500 coins on sign up.
  2. For every referral, the referrer user gets 5, 50 or 500 coins.
  3. All newly minted coins are distributed to all users, however, depending how many referrals a users has made more coins will be allocated for him/her. A user gets 1 share of the pool for singing up, and 1 share for every referral made. Disclosure: Users that do not login into the web-wallet longer than 30 days might loose their participation on the daily distribution. Only the top 1000 referrers will get coins. There might be a cap on the total shares a user can get in the pool for daily distribution.
  4. Coins are awarded to members of the community who contribute to the project.
  5. There are ocassionally give aways to all users to celebrate milestones or special events such as a user year anniversary with Xenblocks.
  6. We will continue to develop fun programs that allow members of the community get rewarded with coins.


5. How can I get additional coins?

The easiest way to get additional coins is by mining Xenblocks. See our mining guide for more information.


6. How do you prevent users from abusing the program?

We continually monitor our programs and when we detect that a user is abusing the program we reserve the right to refuse services and close the abusive accounts.


7. What is the difference between the Web and Desktop wallets?

The web-wallet runs in our servers and users can access it from anywhere. It allows users to view coin balances, send and receive coins and collect daily coin rewards. Desktop wallets are stored in users computers, they are not accessible by our services and users are responsible of keeping their balances and security keys.


8. Are my coins safe in the web-wallet?

We do our best efforts to keep web-wallets secure. However, we cannot guarantee that there could be security breaches in our services, so we recommend all users to frequently transfer their coins form their web-wallets to their own Desktop wallets. As well, as backing up their keys and keeping them in cold storage.


9. Where should I keep my coins?

The web-wallet is a portal to get new coins, send and receive coins, and learn the basics of crypto-currencies. However, for security reasons we recommend every user to store their coins on their own Desktop wallets. We cannot guarantee that there will not be security breaches to our servers that could put users accounts at danger and loose their coins. However, we will do our best efforts to keep our systems as secure as possible.


10. Do Xenblocks (XNB) coins have any value?

No, Xenblocks coins (XNB) currently do not have any value. At this point we don't have any plans to have XNB traded on exchanges. However, we do not rule that possibility out in the future. As that is something that can happen outside our control. In which case, the value of XNB would be set by the free market.
